What is My Hero Academia?


My Hero Academia is an extremely popular anime based off the manga

of the same name. The story takes place in a world where the majoity

of the people have superpowes called quirks. They are called quirks

because everyone's is unique in on way or another. Izuku Midoriya is

the main character and despite being quirkless, he wants to go to UA

Highschool, where the greatest heroes come from.
