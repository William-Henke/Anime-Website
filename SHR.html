What is The Rising of the Shield Hero?


The Rising of the Shield Hero brings together four people,

each fom alternate versions of Japan. They become heroes

and are hailed as champions everywhere they go. Except for

Naofumi Iwatani, the shield hero. For reasons unknown, Naofumi

is despised by just about everyone and suggles to make his way

in this new unfamiliar world.
